About

Hello I'm Andrew and I am a developer currently living in Tuscaloosa, Alabama.

By day I'm an Inventory Manager and a Computer Networking Specialist for a small business in Tuscaloosa. By night I'm working on webapps that will help people to get things done faster, or brushing up on my system administration skills. I enjoy teaching people what I know on the side and enjoy blogging when I remember to do so.

You can find me on Twitter or on other social media sites by looking up Andrew Fomera. If you are also a code-junkie you can find me on GitHub.
